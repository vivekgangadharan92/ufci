About UFCI

UFCI, also known as United Fashion Concepts of India, is a prominent fashion and cosmetics company headquartered in Bangalore, India. Our primary focus lies in the production of high-quality makeup items, luxurious perfumes, and cutting-edge health care products. With a commitment to innovation and a dedication to enhancing beauty and well-being, we are at the forefront of the fashion and cosmetics industry in India. Our mission is to bring forth a diverse range of products that cater to the unique preferences and needs of our discerning customers, ensuring they look and feel their best.

Our Products

Make up

At UFCI, we take pride in creating exquisite makeup products. Our skilled team meticulously crafts a range of makeup items, from richly pigmented eyeshadows to long-lasting lipsticks, all designed to enhance and inspire. Using cutting-edge techniques and premium ingredients, we produce makeup products that define beauty, ensuring every application is a work of art.

Health Care

At UFCI, we are committed to enhancing well-being through our healthcare product range. Our dedicated team formulates a variety of health and wellness items, from dietary supplements to skincare essentials. We prioritize quality and safety, using the latest research and premium ingredients to create products that promote a healthier lifestyle.

Perfumes

UFCI is dedicated to the art of perfumery. Our team of skilled perfumers painstakingly formulates fragrances that captivate the senses. Each of our perfumes is a unique olfactory masterpiece, meticulously crafted with a harmonious blend of top, middle, and base notes. We take pride in producing perfumes that exude elegance, and sophistication, making them the perfect complement.
